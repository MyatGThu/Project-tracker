## 1. Who we are
This Privacy Policy explains how [OPERATOR NAME] ("we", "us") handles personal
information when you use the Poker Tracker application (the "App").

## 2. What we collect
The App is deliberately minimal. We collect only what you enter:

- **Game data** you input — player names, buy-in amounts, final chip counts,
  session names, dates, and notes.
- **Casino visit data** (if used) — casino name, buy-in/cash-out amounts,
  games played, and notes.
- **Local device settings** — your roster, selected mode, and quick-rebuy
  amount, stored in your browser's local storage.

We do **not** collect: email addresses, user accounts, payment or banking
details, real-money gambling transactions, location data, or advertising
identifiers. The App does not run analytics or tracking.

## 3. How we use it
Solely to provide the App's features — tracking games, calculating
settlements, and showing leaderboards, records, and stats. We do not sell,
rent, or share your data for marketing.

## 4. Legal bases (for users in the EU/UK)
We process this data on the basis of your **consent** and our **legitimate
interest** in providing the functionality you requested. You may withdraw
consent at any time by deleting your data or ceasing use.

## 5. Storage & security
- Data is stored in a **Cloudflare D1** database hosted on Cloudflare's
  infrastructure.
- Access to the App is protected by a shared password, verified server-side;
  the password is never stored in the App's code.
- No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect data.

## 6. Third-party services
The App relies on a small number of providers:
- **Cloudflare** — hosting, database, and content delivery.
- **Google Fonts** and **jsDelivr (Chart.js)** — loaded via CDN; these may
  receive your IP address as part of serving fonts/scripts.

We do not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

## 7. Cookies & local storage
The App does not use tracking cookies. It uses your browser's **local storage**
to remember device settings and that you have unlocked the App. Clearing your
browser data removes these.

## 8. Data retention
Game and visit data is retained until you delete it within the App or request
deletion. Backups may persist for up to 30 days (Cloudflare D1 point-in-time
recovery) before being overwritten.

## 9. Your rights

you may request to **access, correct, or delete** your personal information, or

## 10. International users
